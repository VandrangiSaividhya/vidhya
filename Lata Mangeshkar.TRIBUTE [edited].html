Lata Mangeshkar



Indian Playback Singer and Occasional music composer

Here's a time line of Lata Mangeshkar life:

  • ABOUT -

    → Lata Mangeshkar born as Hema Mangeshkar on 28 September 1929 in INDORE. She was an Indian playback singer and occansional music composer.Her contribution to the Indian music industry in a career spanning eight decades gained her honorific tittles such as "Queen of Melody", "Nightangles of India", "Voice of the Millennium."
